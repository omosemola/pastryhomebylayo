Our Story

Crafting Sweet Memories

Welcome to Pastry Home by Layo, where every crumb tells a story. Founded with a simple mission: to bring joy through the art of baking. We believe that a truly great pastry serves the soul as much as the palate.

Our journey began in a small home kitchen, fueled by a passion for creating desserts that not only look stunning but taste unforgettable. Using only the finest ingredients—real butter, fresh cream, and seasonal fruits—we craft treats that are honest, wholesome, and delicious.
